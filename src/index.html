Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?▼
Pressure washing uses high PSI water to clean hard surfaces like concrete and pavers. Soft washing uses low pressure plus cleaning solutions to safely clean delicate surfaces like roofs, screens, stucco, and painted siding. We use the right method for each surface.
How long does paver sealing last?▼
A professional sealant application lasts 2–3 years depending on sun exposure, traffic, and maintenance. We use only high quality sealers selected for your specific paver material and premium joint sand for maximum longevity.
