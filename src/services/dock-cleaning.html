Remove algae, barnacles, and salt buildup from boat docks, seawalls, and waterfront structures.

Cape Coral has over 400 miles of canals, and waterfront properties need regular dock and seawall maintenance. Marine algae, barnacles, salt deposits, and waterline staining accumulate fast — especially during warmer months.

We clean boat docks, fish cleaning stations, seawalls, dock pilings, and waterfront walkways. Our process removes organic growth and marine buildup without damaging dock hardware, boat lifts, or the surrounding waterway.

For canal-front homes, we recommend dock cleaning every 6–12 months. Properties facing open water or the river may need more frequent service due to increased tidal exposure and salt.
