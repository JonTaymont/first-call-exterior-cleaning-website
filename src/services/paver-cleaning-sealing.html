Your paver driveway, walkway, patio, or pool deck is one of the first things people notice about your property. Florida's relentless sun, rain, and humidity fade colors, encourage weed growth in the joints, and attract ant colonies.

Our professional paver cleaning and sealing process starts with a thorough power wash to strip away dirt, algae, mold, and existing failed sealant. We re-sand the joints with premium joint sand to lock everything tight, then apply a high quality sealer selected specifically for your pavers.

The sealer we use depends on factors like paver age, material type, surface texture, and exposure conditions — because the right product for a new travertine pool deck is different from what a 10-year-old concrete paver driveway needs. We only use high quality sealers that deliver lasting results. Protection lasts 2–3 years and guards against UV fading, oil stains, weed growth, and erosion. It's the fastest way to transform your property's curb appeal.
