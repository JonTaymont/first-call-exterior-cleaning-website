Every Florida home has a pool cage or lanai, and they all share the same problem: algae, mildew, pollen, and hard water stains that coat the screens, frames, and ceiling panels. A dirty pool cage doesn't just look bad — it blocks airflow, darkens your outdoor space, and can even affect your pool water quality.

We use a careful low-pressure soft wash technique specifically calibrated for pool cage cleaning. Our process removes organic growth and staining from aluminum frames, screen panels, concrete decking, and pavers without damaging screens, bending frames, or disturbing your landscaping.

Most pool cages in Cape Coral need cleaning every 12–18 months to stay in good shape. If you're near a canal or waterfront, every 6–12 months is better.
