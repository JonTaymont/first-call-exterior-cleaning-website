Fences and retaining walls are some of the most visible parts of your property — and some of the most neglected. In Florida's humidity, vinyl fences turn green with algae in months, wood fences gray out and develop black mold streaks, and stone retaining walls collect organic staining that makes them look decades older than they are.

The challenge with fence and wall cleaning is that every material requires a different approach. High pressure on vinyl can warp panels and blow out seams. Too much pressure on wood splits and splinters the grain. And the wrong chemical on painted surfaces strips the finish.

We match our technique to your material every time. Vinyl and PVC fencing gets a gentle soft wash that removes algae, mold, and grime without warping or damage. Wood fences get calibrated low-pressure treatment that cleans without splintering — and we can stain and seal wood fencing after cleaning to extend its life. Aluminum and wrought iron fences get appropriate pressure with rust-inhibiting treatments where needed. Stone, block, and concrete retaining walls get the pressure they can handle along with targeted treatments for embedded staining.

For HOA communities with consistent fencing throughout the neighborhood, we offer multi-property pricing to keep everything looking uniform. Clean fencing and walls make a measurable difference in curb appeal — it's one of the first things buyers and visitors notice.
