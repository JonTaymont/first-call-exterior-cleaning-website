Exterior Cleaning in North Fort Myers, FL

Professional exterior cleaning, paver sealing, and pressure washing in North Fort Myers and surrounding areas.

North Fort Myers is a mix of established residential neighborhoods, rural properties, and newer developments spread across a large area north of the Caloosahatchee. Large lots, mature trees, and proximity to pine flatwoods mean heavy pollen exposure — that coats every exterior surface.

Properties along the river and in communities like River Hall and Verandah deal with humidity and waterfront conditions. Inland neighborhoods face different challenges: sandy soil, pine pollen, and wide-open sun exposure that accelerates fading on pavers and exterior surfaces.

We service all of North Fort Myers from our Cape Coral base, just across the Midpoint Bridge. Whether you're in a riverfront estate or a quiet neighborhood off Bayshore Road, we bring the same equipment, products, and attention to detail.
