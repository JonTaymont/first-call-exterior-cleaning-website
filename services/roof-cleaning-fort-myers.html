Roof Cleaning for Fort Myers Properties

Those dark streaks running down your Fort Myers roof aren't just cosmetic — they're a type of algae called Gloeocapsa magma that's actively feeding on your roofing material. Left untreated, it shortens your roof's lifespan, increases cooling costs by reducing reflectivity, and makes your home look decades older than it is.

Fort Myers roofs — whether asphalt shingle, concrete tile, clay tile, or metal — all face the same enemy: Florida's combination of heat, humidity, and rain that creates ideal conditions for algae, mold, lichen, and moss.

We use a soft wash roof cleaning method that kills organic growth at the root without damaging your roofing material. No high-pressure blasting that strips granules from shingles or cracks tile. Our biodegradable cleaning solution does the work — the low-pressure rinse just removes the residue.

Results typically last 2–3 years. For Fort Myers homes surrounded by mature trees (especially along McGregor and in Whiskey Creek), you may need more frequent treatment due to increased shade and organic debris.

Roof cleaning also includes gutters, soffits, and fascia — we clean the full roofline so you get a complete transformation, not just a clean roof with dirty edges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is soft washing safe for my Fort Myers tile roof?▼
Yes. Soft washing is the manufacturer-recommended method for cleaning concrete tile, clay tile, and asphalt shingles. We never use high pressure on roofs.
How long do roof cleaning results last?▼
2–3 years on average. Homes with heavy tree cover or north-facing roof planes may see growth return sooner.
Will roof cleaning lower my energy bills?▼
It can help. Dark algae absorbs heat, increasing attic temperatures and making your AC work harder. A clean, reflective roof runs cooler.
