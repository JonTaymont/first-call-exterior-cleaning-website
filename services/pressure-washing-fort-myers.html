Pressure Washing for Fort Myers Properties

Fort Myers properties need pressure washing. It's not a question of if — it's a question of how often. Between the humidity, the rain, the salt air off the Caloosahatchee, and Florida's year-round growing season for algae and mold, every exterior surface in Fort Myers collects grime.

We provide full-service exterior pressure washing and soft washing across Fort Myers — driveways, sidewalks, pool decks, house exteriors, fences, commercial storefronts, parking lots, and everything in between.

What sets us apart is that we don't just blast everything with high pressure. Different surfaces need different approaches. Your stucco gets a gentle soft wash. Your concrete driveway gets full commercial-grade pressure. Your pool cage screens get low-pressure treatment that won't damage the mesh. We match the technique to the material every time.

Fort Myers vacation rental owners: we offer quick-turnaround cleaning between guests. Driveways, walkways, pool decks, and exteriors cleaned and dried before your next check-in. Property managers — ask about our recurring service contracts.

We're in Fort Myers every day from our Cape Coral base. Call for a free estimate — we can usually schedule within the week.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does pressure washing cost in Fort Myers?▼
Every property is different — pricing depends on size, surface type, and condition. We provide free estimates with exact pricing before any work starts. No surprises.
Do you pressure wash commercial properties in Fort Myers?▼
Yes — storefronts, parking lots, sidewalks, building exteriors, and shopping centers. We work early mornings or evenings to avoid disrupting your business.
How is soft washing different from pressure washing?▼
Soft washing uses low pressure plus cleaning solutions to kill algae and mold at the root. It's safer for painted surfaces, stucco, roofs, and screens. We use whichever method is right for the surface.
